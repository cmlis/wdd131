Tia Tea - Website Plan

Site Name

Tia Tea

This name reflects the website's focus on sharing tea history, health benefits, and detailed tea profiles for enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Optional domain: tiatea.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for tea lovers, offering:

  • Profiles and images of different teas.
  • Health and wellness benefits of each tea.
  • Brewing tips and traditional uses.
  • Favorites management and search/filter functionality.

Scenarios

  • Which tea is best for relaxation before bedtime?
  • How can I add my favorite teas to a list for future reference?

Color Schema

Primary colors chosen for site design:

  • Accent (#6b8e23) – used for headings, buttons, and highlights.
  • Muted (#666) – used for body text and secondary information.

Typography

Fonts chosen for consistency and readability:

  • Roboto – used for body text.
  • Roboto Bold – used for headings and buttons.
